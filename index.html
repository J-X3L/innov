About Us

Innovative Technology Solutions Global Nigeria Limited (INNOVTECH) is a private company incorporated in Nigeria & Sierra Leone.

INNOVTECH focuses on innovative technology solutions for the African Agricultural Sector. We aim to bring real and tangible benefit to African communities through technologies which are promoting sustainable agricultural development among smallholders' farmers by improving the livelihood of farmers who have historically struggled to make an adequate living, and reducing poverty rate among the farmers.

Increasing the productivity of both Livestock and crop cultivating farmers, raising the degree of food security of the strategic food commodities, increasing the competitiveness of agricultural products grown with our products for local and international markets.
