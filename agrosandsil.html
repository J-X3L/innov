AGROSAND SIL M20

Ecological protection of plants against insects, diseases and UV radiation

Natural Plant Protection

AgroSand Sil M20 is a form of micronized natural mineral - clinoptilolite, which forms a barrier film on the plant, that acts as a broad-spectrum preservative. It uses the properties of zeolite for effective nutrition and plant protection.

How it works: Proper application creates a micronized film on the leaf surface, which is an inhospitable environment for insects, diseases and pests. At the same time, it protects the plant from UV radiation.

Key Benefits

  • Strengthening of cell walls
  • Increased resistance of plants to environmental stress
  • Increased resistance to diseases and fungi
  • Protection against pests
  • Increased metabolic functions of plants
  • Increases the effect of biopesticides by 30%

Protection Mechanisms

🛡️
Physical Barrier

Creates a mineral film that physically blocks pests from accessing plant surfaces while allowing gas exchange.

☀️
UV Protection

Reflects harmful UV radiation while allowing beneficial light wavelengths to pass through.

🦠
Microbial Support

Protects beneficial microorganisms from UV damage and provides habitat for their proliferation.

Product Composition

  • Silica Content: 60% of total composition
  • Additional Minerals: Calcium, iron, magnesium, potassium, manganese and titanium
  • Granulometry: Particles below 20 microns
  • Form: Fine powder

Application Guide

  • Dosage: 0.5 - 1 kg/ha mixed with 300 liters of water
  • Timing: Apply during early morning or late afternoon
  • Mixing: Ensure constant agitation during application
  • Compatibility: Works well with most fertilizers and biopesticides
  • Recommendation: Use a wetting agent for best results
  • Packaging: 15 kg bags
