PURE GROWTH PLUS

Unique natural foliar fertilizer for enhanced plant growth and stress resistance

Nature's Growth Catalyst

Pure growth Plus is a natural mineral fertilizer that uses its powerful natural components to stimulate processes in the plants that promote higher volume and enhanced quality of production. This purely natural product is free from chemical substances and allows obtaining harvest with excellent biological qualities for small gardeners, as well as large agricultural producers.

Key Benefits

  • Increased anti-stress resistance during dry/hot periods
  • Significantly increased harvest volumes
  • Improved nutritional content (proteins, sugars)
  • Prolonged storage of harvested crops
  • Enhanced efficiency of fertilizers and pesticides
  • Improved plant immunity and health
  • Better water management in plants

How Pure Growth Plus Works

Stress Resistance

Helps plants close pores to reduce water evaporation, making them more resilient to drought and temperature extremes.

Yield & Quality

Boosts both quantity and quality of harvests, improving coloration, taste, and nutritional value of produce.

Growth Support

Accelerates photosynthesis and optimizes internal plant processes for vigorous growth and early fruiting.

Plant Health

Strengthens plant immunity and resistance to pathogens while enhancing pesticide effectiveness.

Water Management

Improves plants' ability to regulate water content and survive periods of water stress.

Universal Application

Suitable for nearly all crops.

Application Guidelines

Apply minimum 2 times during growing period by spraying on leaves:

  • Field Crops: 1.5 - 2 kg/ha + min. 300 l water/ha
  • Ornamentals, Fruits & Vegetables: 1.5 - 2 kg/ha + min. 400 l water/ha
Note: For best results, apply during cooler parts of the day (early morning or late afternoon) and avoid application before expected rainfall.

Compatibility

Pure Growth Plus is compatible with:

  • Most foliar fertilizers
  • Pesticides (check specific product compatibility)
  • Biopesticides and organic inputs
  • Micronutrient solutions

Note: Always conduct a jar test before mixing with other products.
