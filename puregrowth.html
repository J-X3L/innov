Application Guidelines

Suitable for all crops except acid-loving plants. Particularly effective on:

  • Fruits (grapes, apples, citrus, berries)
  • Vegetables (tomatoes, peppers, leafy greens)
  • Field crops (corn, wheat, soybeans)
  • Root crops (potatoes, sugar beets, carrots)
  • Ornamentals and nursery stock

Crop Type Rate Water
Field Crops 1.5-2 kg/ha 300 L/ha
Fruits & Vegetables 1.5-2 kg/ha 400 L/ha
Ornamentals 1.5-2 kg/ha 400 L/ha

  • Apply minimum 2 times during growing season
  • First application at early growth stage
  • Second application at flowering/fruit set
  • Additional applications may benefit during stress periods
  • Apply during cooler parts of the day (morning/evening)
  • Avoid application before expected rainfall
