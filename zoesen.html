ZEOSEN LIVESTOCK

Cracking The Livestock Performance Code

WHAT IS ZEOSEN LIVESTOCK

Zeosen Livestock is pure nature, it is made from clinoptilolite (zeolite), a mineral of volcanic origin. Clinoptilolite was used from the start in the past, first to reduce the biting smell of ammonia in the barn. The application was a complete success, not only improved the air, but a whole range of positive effects.

Zeosen Livestock is manufactured in a special advanced technology micronization with patented technology, enhancing the immune system, antioxidant enzymes, and promoting digestibility and bioavailability of other nutrients.

Key Features
  • Ion-exchangers and adsorbents for animal feed
  • Acts as antacids and drugs (Enterex)
  • Effective anti-caking agent
  • Manufactured under EU-Directive
  • Veterinary Approved 100% Natural

MICRONIZATION EFFECTS

  • Reduced particle size: 200nm-10μm and increased specific surface
  • Increased mesoporosity: portion of total mesoporous surface within BET total surface exceeds 50%
  • Due to mentioned characteristics, reactive portion of particle surface is increased, thus stronger interaction with biological tissues

MECHANISM OF ACTION

Adsorption of Bacteria Toxins - Due to its small size, Zeosen interacts via epithelia cell (Villi) and probiotic bacteria, thus modulating immune system enzymes, and performing detoxification.

TOXIN REMOVAL

Zeosen efficiently adsorbs and immobilizes:

  • Mycotoxins (ochratoxin and aflatoxin B1)
  • Nitrosamines
  • Dioxines
  • Heavy metals (Pb, Hg, Cd, As) and Ammonia
  • Bacterial toxins and viruses
IMMUNE MODULATION

Zeosen interacts in gait which induces:

  • Increased Macrophage and DC response
  • CD4 CD8 proliferation
  • Improvement of gut cellular barrier
SILICON SUPPLEMENTATION

MZ is a highly effective source of bioavailable silicon (H4SiO4), therefore promoting:

  • Collagen and elastin synthesis
  • Faster bone growth and mineralization
  • Improved tendon strength (reduction of injury)
  • Faster growth, thicker and lustre hair (coat, fur)

ANTIOXIDANT EFFECT

  • SOD, Catalase and peroxidase enzymes are stimulated by MZ therefore reducing oxidation, peroxidation and lipid peroxidation.
  • MZ absorbs hydroxyl radical molecule which wreaks havoc in the body.
